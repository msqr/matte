Ansel Adams Wilderness

6 Jun 2006 - 28 items - Last updated 28 Jun 2006
Minette and I hiked into the Ansel Adams Wilderness, slightly before the trails were officially open. We started from the gate that blocked the road a couple of miles from Reds Meadow and hiked up to Shadow Lake, followed by Ediza Lake, followed by Thousand Island Lake. It was a bit snowy in places, and cold at night, but beautiful by day.
